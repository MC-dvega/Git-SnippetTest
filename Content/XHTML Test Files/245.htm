Wireless

The Mood Mouse is a wireless device.

Before taking your Mood Mouse on an airplane, make sure you remove the batteries. The Mood Mouse can emit radio frequency (RF) energy, much like a cellular telephone, when the batteries are installed, even if the Mood Mouse is turned off.

You can monitor the following settings:

  • Battery Status
  • Transmission Channel

You can also run the Mood Mouse Troubleshooter to help resolve problems.
