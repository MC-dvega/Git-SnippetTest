Battery Status

Indicates the battery power level for the Mood Mouse. The palm rest changes colors after it hasn't been used for 30 minutes:

  • Light purple (good). Batteries do not need replacing.
  • Medium purple (medium). Have new batteries ready.
  • Dark purple (low). Replace batteries.
  • White (unknown). No information received. Use the Mood Mouse Troubleshooter to resolve the problems.
