Product Description

The Mood Mouse is a revolutionary computing tool that combines the efficiency of a wireless mouse with an emotional indicator, much like the mood rings of the 1970s. The palm rest registers the heat in your palm and the buttons take your pulse to determine your mood. The palm rest changes to the appropriate color and Moodicator messages are displayed to let you know when you should take a walk, drink a cup of tea, or call a loved one. A thumb button lets you quickly disable the indicators in case someone walks into your office.

When you put your hand on the Mood Mouse, the palm rest changes color to indicate your mood and a color-coded message is displayed.

  • Dark blue: Happy, romantic or passionate. Call a loved one!
  • Blue: Calm or relaxed. Have a glass of lemonade.
  • Blue-green: Somewhat relaxed. Enjoy a snack!
  • Green: Normal or average. Keep up the good work!
  • Amber: A little nervous or anxious. Go for a walk!
  • Gray: Very nervous or anxious. Have a nice cup of tea.
  • Black: Stressed, tense or feeling harried. Better leave a little early today!
  • Purple: Battery Status

You can't change the colors or color definitions.

The Mood Mouse is optical, wireless, symmetrical, and ambidextrous. It can use either a USB or PS/2 port.
